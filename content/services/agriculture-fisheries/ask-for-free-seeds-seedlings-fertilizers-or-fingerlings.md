# Free Seeds, Seedlings, and Fertilizers — Bacolod City

The **City Department of Agriculture** provides free agricultural inputs to qualified farmers and urban gardeners in Bacolod City.

---

## 1. Available Agricultural Inputs

| Input Type         | Availability            | For                               |
| ------------------ | ----------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Vegetable seeds    | Subject to availability | Backyard gardeners, urban farmers |
| Seedlings          | Subject to availability | Farmers, community gardens        |
| Fertilizers        | Limited supply          | Registered farmers                |
| Fingerlings (fish) | Subject to availability | Fishpond owners, aquaculture      |

---

## 2. Who Can Avail

**Eligible Beneficiaries:**

- Registered farmers in Bacolod City
- Backyard gardeners
- Urban farming practitioners
- Community garden groups
- Barangay-based agricultural cooperatives

---

## 3. Requirements

- Valid ID
- Proof of Bacolod residency (barangay certificate)
- Proof of farming activity (for registered farmers)
- Barangay endorsement (for community projects)

---

## 4. How to Apply

1. Visit **City Department of Agriculture** office
2. Inquire about available inputs
3. Submit requirements
4. Fill out request form
5. Wait for approval and schedule of distribution
6. Claim inputs at designated location

## 6. Important Reminders

- Inputs are distributed on a **first-come, first-served** basis
- Supply is **limited** - check availability before visiting
- Priority given to registered farmers and organized groups
- Bring valid ID and proof of residency

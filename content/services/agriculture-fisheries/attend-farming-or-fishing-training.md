# Farming and Fishing Training — Bacolod City

The **City Department of Agriculture** and **City Veterinary Office** offer training programs for farmers, fisherfolk, and urban gardeners.

---

## 1. Training Programs Available

| Training Type         | Topics Covered                                               |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Urban gardening       | Container gardening, vertical farming, composting            |
| Vegetable production  | Planting techniques, pest management, harvesting             |
| Livestock raising     | Swine, poultry, goat raising, animal health                  |
| Aquaculture           | Fish pond management, tilapia culture, fingerling production |
| Organic farming       | Natural fertilizers, pesticide-free methods                  |
| Post-harvest handling | Storage, processing, value-adding                            |

---

## 2. Who Can Join

- Farmers and aspiring farmers
- Fisherfolk and aquaculture practitioners
- Backyard gardeners
- Barangay agricultural groups
- Cooperatives and associations
- Students (agriculture-related courses)
- Bacolod City residents interested in farming

---

## 3. How to Join

1. Contact **City Department of Agriculture** for training schedule
2. Register for desired training program
3. Submit requirements (valid ID, proof of residency)
4. Attend training on scheduled date
5. Receive certificate of completion

---

## 4. Training Schedule

- Training schedules vary throughout the year
- Contact City Agriculture Office for upcoming trainings
- Some trainings are conducted at barangay level
- Special trainings may be arranged for groups

---

## 5. Requirements

- Valid ID
- Proof of Bacolod residency
- Registration form (from City Agriculture)
- For group trainings: Letter request from organization

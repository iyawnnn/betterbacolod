# Veterinary Services for Livestock — Bacolod City

The **City Veterinary Office** provides veterinary services for livestock, poultry, and farm animals in Bacolod City.

---

## 1. Services Available

| Service                    | Description                                                |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Animal health consultation | Diagnosis and treatment advice for sick animals            |
| Vaccination                | Rabies, hog cholera, Newcastle disease, and other vaccines |
| Deworming                  | Parasite control for livestock                             |
| Artificial insemination    | Breeding services for cattle and swine                     |
| Meat inspection            | Inspection of slaughtered animals for public health        |
| Animal registration        | Registration of livestock and pets                         |

---

## 2. Who Can Avail

- Livestock owners (cattle, carabao, swine, goats)
- Poultry raisers (chickens, ducks)
- Pet owners (dogs, cats - for rabies vaccination)
- Farmers with farm animals
- Bacolod City residents

---

## 3. Requirements

**For Veterinary Consultation:**

- Valid ID
- Bring the animal or describe symptoms
- Previous medical records (if available)

**For Vaccination:**

- Valid ID
- Animal to be vaccinated
- Proof of ownership (for livestock)

**For Meat Inspection:**

- Slaughterhouse permit
- Animals for slaughter

---

## 4. How to Avail

1. Visit **City Veterinary Office** or contact by phone
2. Describe your concern or service needed
3. Schedule appointment (if needed)
4. Bring animal and requirements
5. Receive service
6. Pay fees (if applicable)

---

## 5. Fees

| Service                 | Fee                                    |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Consultation            | Free to minimal                        |
| Rabies vaccination      | Free (during campaigns) or minimal fee |
| Other vaccinations      | Varies by vaccine                      |
| Artificial insemination | Subsidized rates                       |
| Meat inspection         | Per head fee                           |

**Note:** Some services are free during government campaigns. Contact office for current fees.

## 7. Important Reminders

- **Rabies vaccination** is mandatory for dogs and cats
- Report animal disease outbreaks immediately
- Proper animal handling during transport
- Follow quarantine protocols for sick animals
- Meat must be inspected before sale

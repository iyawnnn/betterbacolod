# Borrow Farm Tools and Equipment — Bacolod City

The **City Department of Agriculture** provides farm tools and equipment lending services to qualified farmers in Bacolod City.

---

## 1. Equipment Available for Borrowing

| Equipment Type         | Examples                                    |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Hand tools             | Shovels, hoes, rakes, bolos, pruning shears |
| Sprayers               | Knapsack sprayers, power sprayers           |
| Post-harvest equipment | Threshers, dryers (subject to availability) |
| Irrigation equipment   | Pumps, hoses (subject to availability)      |

**Note:** Availability varies. Contact City Agriculture Office for current inventory.

---

## 2. Who Can Borrow

**Eligible Borrowers:**

- Registered farmers in Bacolod City
- Farmers' cooperatives and associations
- Barangay agricultural groups
- Community garden projects

---

## 3. Requirements

- Valid ID
- Proof of Bacolod residency (barangay certificate)
- Proof of farming activity
- Barangay endorsement (for groups)
- Signed borrower's agreement

---

## 4. How to Borrow

1. Visit **City Department of Agriculture** office
2. Inquire about available equipment
3. Submit requirements
4. Fill out borrower's agreement form
5. Schedule pickup and return dates
6. Return equipment in good condition

---

## 5. Terms and Conditions

- Equipment must be returned on agreed date
- Borrower is responsible for equipment during use
- Damaged or lost equipment must be replaced/paid for
- Equipment is for agricultural use only
- Priority given to registered farmers and cooperatives

## 7. Important Reminders

- **Reserve early** - equipment is limited
- **Return on time** - other farmers may be waiting
- **Handle with care** - you are responsible for the equipment
- **Report damage** - inform office immediately if equipment is damaged
- **No subletting** - do not lend to others

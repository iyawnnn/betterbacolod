# Senior Citizen Benefits, Discounts & Assistance — Bacolod City

Senior citizens (60 years old and above) in Bacolod City are entitled to various benefits, discounts, and assistance programs under **Republic Act 9994 (Expanded Senior Citizens Act)** and local ordinances.

---

## 1) Senior Citizen Benefits & Privileges

### Mandatory Discounts (20%)

Senior citizens are entitled to a **20% discount** on the following:

| Category                | Items/Services                                                    | Notes                                |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **Food Establishments** | Restaurants, fast food, cafes                                     | At least one meal per day            |
| **Transportation**      | Jeepneys, buses, taxis, domestic flights, sea vessels             | Valid for regular trips              |
| **Hotels & Lodging**    | Hotels, resorts, pension houses                                   | Must show valid ID                   |
| **Entertainment**       | Movies, theaters, concerts, museums                               | Valid ID required                    |
| **Medicines**           | Prescription and over-the-counter medicines                       | At all drugstores                    |
| **Medical Services**    | Professional fees (doctors, dentists), diagnostic tests, lab fees | In private facilities                |
| **Funeral Services**    | Funeral parlors, burial services                                  | For the senior citizen's own funeral |

### VAT Exemption

Senior citizens are **exempt from 12% VAT** on purchases of goods and services for personal use (up to ₱1,300 weekly for essential goods).

## 2) How to Get a Senior Citizen ID

### Requirements

- Must be **60 years old or above**
- Valid government-issued ID (PhilSys, passport, driver's license, etc.)
- Birth certificate or PSA birth certificate
- 1×1 ID photo (2 copies)
- Barangay Clearance or Certificate of Residency
- Accomplished application form (available at barangay or DSSD)

### Where to Apply

**Option 1: Barangay Level (Recommended)**

1. Go to your **Barangay Hall**
2. Ask for the **Senior Citizen Affairs Desk**
3. Submit requirements
4. Pay processing fee (if any)
5. Receive Senior Citizen ID

**Option 2: City Level (DSSD)**

- **Department of Social Services and Development (DSSD)**
- **Email:** dssd@bacolodcity.gov.ph
- **Phone:** (034) 432-1602
- **Location:** Bacolod City Government Center, Circumferential Rd, Brgy. Villamonte, Bacolod City

### Processing Time

- Barangay level: Same day to 1-3 days
- City level: 3-5 working days

## 3) Additional Benefits & Programs

### Social Pension for Indigent Senior Citizens

**Eligibility:**

- 60 years old and above
- **Indigent** (no regular income, no pension)
- Not receiving any other government pension

**Benefit:** Monthly cash assistance (amount varies; currently ₱500/month under DSWD program)

**How to Apply:**

1. Apply at your **Barangay Hall** or **DSSD**
2. Submit proof of indigency (Barangay Certification, DSWD assessment)
3. Submit Senior Citizen ID and valid IDs
4. Wait for assessment and approval

### Free Medical Assistance

- Free consultations and medicines at **Bacolod City Health Centers**
- Priority lane at hospitals and clinics
- PhilHealth coverage for senior citizens

### PhilHealth Benefits

- Senior citizens can avail of **free PhilHealth coverage** if not currently a member
- Register at PhilHealth offices or online
- Coverage includes hospitalization, outpatient services, and consultations

---

## 4) How to Avail Discounts

### When Making Purchases or Transactions

1. **Present your Senior Citizen ID** before payment
2. Inform the cashier/staff that you are availing the senior citizen discount
3. Discount will be deducted from your bill
4. Keep receipts for your records

### Tips

- Always carry your Senior Citizen ID
- Some establishments may require additional valid ID
- Discounts apply only to the senior citizen's own consumption (not for group purchases)
- If refused, you can report violations to DSSD or DTI

## 6) Reporting Violations

If an establishment **refuses to give discounts** or violates senior citizen rights:

**Report to:**

- **DSSD Office** — (034) 432-1602 or dssd@bacolodcity.gov.ph
- **Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)** — for business violations
- **Barangay Officials** — for local mediation

# PWD ID Registration & Disability Benefits — Bacolod City

Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) in Bacolod City are entitled to benefits, discounts, and assistance under **Republic Act 7277 (Magna Carta for Persons with Disability)** as amended by **RA 10754** and **RA 11228 (PWD ID Act)**.

---

## 1) Who Qualifies as a PWD?

Persons with **long-term or permanent physical, mental, intellectual, or sensory impairments** that substantially limit one or more major life activities, including:

- Physical disability (mobility impairments, amputations, etc.)
- Visual impairment (blind, low vision)
- Hearing impairment (deaf, hard of hearing)
- Speech and language impairment
- Intellectual disability (cognitive impairments)
- Mental/psychosocial disability
- Learning disabilities
- Chronic illnesses (if substantially limiting)

---

## 2) PWD Benefits & Privileges

### Mandatory Discounts (20%)

PWDs are entitled to a **20% discount** on:

| Category                | Items/Services                                                    | Notes                     |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| **Food Establishments** | Restaurants, fast food, cafes                                     | At least one meal per day |
| **Transportation**      | Jeepneys, buses, taxis, domestic flights, sea vessels             | Must show valid PWD ID    |
| **Hotels & Lodging**    | Hotels, resorts, pension houses                                   | Valid ID required         |
| **Entertainment**       | Movies, theaters, concerts, museums                               | Valid PWD ID required     |
| **Medicines**           | Prescription and over-the-counter medicines                       | At all drugstores         |
| **Medical Services**    | Professional fees (doctors, dentists), diagnostic tests, lab fees | In private facilities     |
| **Funeral Services**    | Funeral parlors, burial services                                  | For the PWD's own funeral |

### VAT Exemption

PWDs are **exempt from 12% VAT** on purchases of goods and services for personal use.

### Other Benefits

- **Priority lanes** at government offices, banks, transportation terminals
- **Free PhilHealth coverage** (if not currently covered)
- **Employment assistance** and skills training
- **Educational assistance** (scholarships, subsidies)
- **Accessibility accommodations** in public spaces

## 3) How to Apply for PWD ID

### Requirements

1. **Accomplished PWD Application Form** (available at barangay or DSSD)
2. **Medical Certificate** from a licensed physician certifying the disability
   - Must specify type of disability and permanence
   - Valid for 6 months from date of issuance
3. **Valid government-issued ID** (PhilSys, passport, driver's license, birth certificate)
4. **1×1 or 2×2 ID photo** (2 copies)
5. **Barangay Clearance** or Certificate of Residency
6. **Birth Certificate** (if no other valid ID)

### Where to Apply

**Option 1: Barangay Level**

1. Go to your **Barangay Hall**
2. Ask for the **PWD Affairs Desk** or Social Services
3. Submit requirements
4. Barangay will forward application to City Social Welfare Office for validation
5. Receive PWD ID (processing time: 5-10 working days)

**Option 2: City Level (DSSD)**

- **Department of Social Services and Development (DSSD)**
- **Email:** dssd@bacolodcity.gov.ph
- **Phone:** (034) 432-1602
- **Location:** Bacolod City Government Center, Circumferential Rd, Brgy. Villamonte, Bacolod City

### Processing Time

- Barangay coordination + City validation: **5-10 working days**
- Direct application at DSSD: **7-14 working days**

### Fees

- **FREE** — No application or processing fees under RA 11228

---

## 4) PWD ID Validity & Renewal

- PWD IDs are **valid for 5 years**
- Renewal process is similar to initial application
- Must renew before expiration to continue availing benefits
- Keep your PWD ID updated with current information

---

## 5) Additional Assistance Programs for PWDs

### Social Pension for Indigent PWDs

**Eligibility:**

- 18 years old and above
- Indigent PWD (no regular income, no pension)
- Not receiving other government pension

**Benefit:** Monthly cash assistance (currently ₱500/month under DSWD program)

**How to Apply:**

1. Apply at **Barangay Hall** or **DSSD**
2. Submit proof of indigency (Barangay Certification, DSWD assessment)
3. Submit PWD ID and valid IDs
4. Wait for assessment and approval

### Skills Training & Livelihood Programs

- DSSD coordinates with TESDA and other agencies for skills training
- Livelihood programs for PWD entrepreneurs
- Job matching and employment assistance

### Educational Assistance

- Scholarship programs for PWD students
- Subsidies for school supplies and tuition
- Check with DSSD for available programs

### Assistive Devices & Rehabilitation

- Assistive devices (wheelchairs, crutches, hearing aids, etc.) may be available through DSSD or partner organizations
- Physical therapy and rehabilitation services at Bacolod City Health Centers

---

## 6) How to Avail PWD Discounts

### When Making Purchases or Transactions

1. **Present your PWD ID** before payment
2. Inform the cashier/staff that you are availing the PWD discount
3. Discount will be deducted from your bill
4. Keep receipts for your records

### Tips

- Always carry your PWD ID
- Discounts apply only to the PWD's own consumption
- If refused, report violations to DSSD or DTI

**Report to:**

- **DSSD Office** — (034) 432-1602 or dssd@bacolodcity.gov.ph
- **Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)** — for business violations
- **National Council on Disability Affairs (NCDA)** — for serious violations

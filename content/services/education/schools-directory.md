# Schools in Bacolod City (Public & Private Directory)

Find elementary schools, high schools, and colleges in Bacolod City.

---

## Public Schools

Public schools in Bacolod are under the **DepEd Division of Bacolod City**.

### Public High Schools

| School                            | Notes                                                                                                          |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Negros Occidental High School** | Oldest public secondary school in the province (founded 1902). Offers STEM, Arts, Journalism, Sports programs. |
| **Bata National High School**     | Established 1967. Located at the Bacolod-Talisay boundary.                                                     |

### Public Elementary Schools

| School                      | Notes                                                  |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Rizal Elementary School** | One of the oldest public elementary schools in Bacolod |

> **Note:** Bacolod City has 50+ public elementary schools and 10+ public high schools. For the complete list, contact DepEd Bacolod or visit their office.

## Enrollment Tips

- **Public schools:** Enrollment is usually April-May. Bring PSA birth certificate, report card, and barangay certificate.
- **Private schools:** Contact schools directly for entrance exams and requirements.
- **Transferees:** Request Form 137 (school records) from your previous school.

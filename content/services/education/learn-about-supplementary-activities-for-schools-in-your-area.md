# School Clubs & Student Activities in Bacolod City

For school clubs, student organizations, and extracurricular activities, check with your school or DepEd Bacolod.

---

## Where to Find Information

| Source                          | What to Check                       |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| **Your school's Facebook page** | Club tryouts, org announcements     |
| **DepEd Bacolod City Division** | Division-wide contests, events      |
| **Barangay SK Office**          | Sangguniang Kabataan youth programs |

---

## Pasidungog Awards

The city honors outstanding student athletes and academic achievers through the annual **Pasidungog Awards**.

- Watch for announcements from Bacolod City Government
- Schools nominate qualified students

# Bacolod-Silay International Airport

The main airport serving Bacolod City and Negros Occidental.

---

## Airport Information

- **Airport Code:** BCD (IATA) / RPVB (ICAO)
- **Location:** Barangay Bagtic, Silay City, Negros Occidental
- **Distance from Bacolod:** 15 km northeast (~30 minutes by car)
- **Operator:** Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines (CAAP)
- **Opened:** January 18, 2008

## Getting to the Airport

### Airport Shuttle (Basia Transport)

- **Fare:** ₱130 per person
- **Travel time:** ~30 minutes from Bacolod
- **Website:** [basiaairportshuttle.wordpress.com](https://basiaairportshuttle.wordpress.com)

### Other Options

- **Taxi/Grab:** Available from Bacolod City
- **Private vehicle:** Via Airport Road, Silay City
- **Jeepney:** Bacolod to Silay, then tricycle to airport

---

## Airport Facilities

- Single terminal (domestic and international capable)
- Restaurants and shops
- ATMs
- Parking area
- Taxi and shuttle services

---

## Tips

- Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights
- Check-in counters open 3 hours before departure
- Bring valid ID for check-in
- Check airline baggage allowances before packing

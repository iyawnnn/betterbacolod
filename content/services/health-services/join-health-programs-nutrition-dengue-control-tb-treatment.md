# Join Health Programs — Bacolod City (Nutrition, Dengue Control, TB Treatment)

Bacolod City offers various **health programs** through the City Health Office and barangay health centers.

---

## 1. Nutrition Programs

### For Children

- **Supplementary feeding programs**
- **Vitamin A supplementation**
- **Deworming**

### For Pregnant/Lactating Women

- **Iron and folic acid supplements**
- **Nutrition counseling**

**Where**: Barangay Health Centers  
**Schedule**: Check with your local health center

---

## 2. Dengue Control & Prevention

- **Misting/fogging** in affected areas
- **Clean-up drives** (4S strategy: Search and destroy, Self-protection, Seek early consultation, Say no to indiscriminate fogging)
- **Health education** on dengue prevention

**Report dengue cases**: City Health Office (034) 434-9122

---

## 3. TB Treatment (DOTS Program)

### Free TB Services

- **TB screening and diagnosis**
- **Free TB medicines** (6-month treatment)
- **Regular monitoring** by health workers

**Where**: City Health Office, Barangay Health Centers  
**Requirements**: Sputum test, chest X-ray (if needed)

---

## How to Join

1. **Visit** your Barangay Health Center or City Health Office
2. **Register** for the program you need
3. **Follow** the schedule for check-ups and services

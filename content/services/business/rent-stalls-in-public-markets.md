# Rent Stalls in Public Markets — Bacolod City

Bacolod City operates public markets where vendors can rent stalls for their businesses.

---

## How to Rent a Market Stall

### Requirements

- Valid ID
- Barangay Clearance
- Business Permit (or application in progress)
- Proof of residency

### Process

1. **Visit the market** you're interested in
2. **Inquire at the Market Administrator's Office** about available stalls
3. **Submit requirements** and application form
4. **Pay rental fees** (rates vary by market and stall type)
5. **Sign contract** and start operations

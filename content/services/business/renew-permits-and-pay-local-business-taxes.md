# Renew Business Permits and Pay Local Business Taxes — Bacolod City

All businesses operating in Bacolod City must renew their business permits annually and pay local business taxes.

---

## Business One-Stop Shop (BOSS)

**For Fast Renewal**: Visit BOSS at Bacolod City Government Center lobby  
**Extended until**: March 31, 2026  
**Services**: One-stop processing for all permit requirements

---

## Requirements for Renewal

- Previous year's Business Permit
- Official Receipt of last payment
- Updated documents (if business changed)
- Community Tax Certificate (CTC)
- Clearances (Fire, Sanitation, if applicable)

---

## How to Renew

1. **Prepare requirements** listed above
2. **Go to BOSS** at Bacolod City Government Center
3. **Submit at BPLO counter** for assessment
4. **Pay fees** at Treasurer's Office
5. **Claim renewed permit**

---

## Payment Options

- **In-person**: Treasurer's Office at BOSS/City Hall
- **Online**: Check [onlineservices.bacolodcity.gov.ph](https://onlineservices.bacolodcity.gov.ph/)

---

## Important Dates

- **Deadline**: Typically January 20 (check current year deadline)
- **Penalty**: Late renewals incur surcharges
- **BOSS Extended**: Until March 31, 2026

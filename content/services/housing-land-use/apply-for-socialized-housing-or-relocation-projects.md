# Socialized Housing and Relocation — Bacolod City

The **Bacolod Housing Authority (BHA)** manages socialized housing programs and relocation projects for qualified beneficiaries.

---

## 1. Housing Programs Available

| Program                    | Description                                      | Target Beneficiaries                       |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| Socialized housing         | Affordable housing units for low-income families | Indigent families, informal settlers       |
| Relocation projects        | Resettlement sites for displaced families        | Families affected by demolition, disasters |
| Community Mortgage Program | Financing assistance for land acquisition        | Organized community associations           |
| Asenso Yuhum Village       | Housing project in Brgy. Vista Alegre            | 4Ps beneficiaries, qualified families      |

---

## 2. Eligibility Requirements

**General Qualifications:**

- Filipino citizen
- Resident of Bacolod City (at least 1 year)
- Low-income family (income below poverty threshold)
- No existing housing unit or lot
- Not a beneficiary of other government housing programs
- Willing to comply with program requirements

**Priority Beneficiaries:**

- Informal settlers in danger zones
- Families affected by government projects (road widening, etc.)
- Disaster-affected families
- 4Ps (Pantawid Pamilyang Pilipino Program) beneficiaries
- Senior citizens, PWDs, solo parents

---

## 3. How to Apply

### Step 1: Inquire at BHA

- Visit **Bacolod Housing Authority** office
- Ask about available housing programs
- Get list of requirements

### Step 2: Prepare Requirements

**Common Requirements:**

- Accomplished application form
- Valid IDs (husband and wife)
- Marriage certificate (if married)
- Birth certificates of family members
- Certificate of Indigency from Barangay
- Proof of Bacolod residency (barangay certificate, voter's ID)
- Income documents (payslip, ITR, or affidavit of income)
- 2×2 ID photos (family members)

### Step 3: Submit Application

- Submit complete requirements to BHA
- Attend interview/assessment
- Wait for evaluation and approval

### Step 4: Await Selection

- BHA evaluates applications
- Qualified applicants are listed
- Selection based on priority and availability
- Applicants are notified of results

---

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

**Socialized Housing:**

- Monthly amortization: ₱500-₱2,000 (depending on unit)
- Payment period: 20-25 years
- No interest or minimal interest

**Community Mortgage Program:**

- Financing through government agencies (SHFC, Pag-IBIG)
- Terms vary by program

## 7. Important Reminders

- **Apply early** - housing programs have limited slots
- **Be patient** - processing and construction take time
- **Attend orientations** - BHA conducts pre-occupancy seminars
- **Comply with requirements** - failure to comply may disqualify application
- **No fixers** - application is free, beware of scammers

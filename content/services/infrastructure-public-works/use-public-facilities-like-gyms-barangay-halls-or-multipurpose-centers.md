# Public Facilities Access — Bacolod City

Access and use public facilities including gyms, barangay halls, multipurpose centers, parks, and sports facilities in Bacolod City.

---

## 1. Public Facilities Available

| Facility Type        | Examples                                       | Managed By                 |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Barangay halls       | Community halls in all 61 barangays            | Barangay government        |
| Sports facilities    | Panaad Stadium, barangay courts, gyms          | City government, barangays |
| Parks and plazas     | Public Plaza, Panaad Park, barangay parks      | City government            |
| Multipurpose centers | Community centers, covered courts              | Barangay government        |
| Public markets       | Central Market, Libertad Market, Burgos Market | City government            |

---

## 2. How to Use Public Facilities

### Barangay Halls

**Available for:**

- Community meetings
- Barangay events
- Private events (weddings, birthdays, etc.)

**How to Reserve:**

1. Visit your **Barangay Hall**
2. Ask for facility reservation form
3. Submit requirements (valid ID, purpose of use)
4. Pay rental fee (if applicable)
5. Receive confirmation and schedule

**Fees:** Free to minimal (₱500-₱2,000 depending on barangay)

### Sports Facilities

**Panaad Stadium:**

- For sports events, tournaments, concerts
- Contact **City Sports Office** or **Panaad management**
- Rental fees apply for commercial events

**Barangay Courts/Gyms:**

- Usually free for residents
- Reserve through Barangay Hall
- Priority for barangay activities

### Parks and Plazas

**Public Plaza (City Center):**

- Open to public (free)
- For events: coordinate with City government
- No reservation needed for casual visits

**Panaad Park:**

- Open to public
- Entrance fee may apply for special areas
- Contact Panaad management for events

### Multipurpose Centers

- Available for community activities
- Reserve through Barangay Hall
- Free to minimal rental fee

---

## 3. Requirements for Facility Use

**For Personal/Private Events:**

- Accomplished reservation form
- Valid IDs
- Purpose of use
- Proof of residency (for barangay facilities)
- Deposit (refundable, if required)

**For Commercial/Large Events:**

- Business permit
- Event permit from City government
- Insurance (for large events)
- Security plan
- Sanitation plan

---

## 4. Rules and Regulations

**General Rules:**

- Keep facilities clean
- No smoking in covered areas
- No alcoholic beverages (unless permitted)
- No gambling
- Respect facility hours
- Return facilities in good condition

**Prohibited:**

- Vandalism or damage to property
- Illegal activities
- Disturbing the peace
- Littering

---

## 7. Important Reminders

- **Reserve early** - popular dates book quickly
- **Confirm reservation** - verify schedule before event
- **Clean up after use** - leave facility as you found it
- **Report damage** - inform barangay of any damage
- **Follow rules** - respect facility regulations
- **Be considerate** - keep noise levels reasonable

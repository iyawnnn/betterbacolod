# Public Markets and Cemeteries — Bacolod City

Rent stalls in public markets or use LGU-managed cemetery services in Bacolod City.

---

## 2. How to Rent Market Stalls

### Requirements:

- Accomplished application form
- Valid government-issued IDs
- Barangay clearance
- Business permit (or intent to apply)
- Mayor's permit (or intent to apply)
- Sanitary permit (from City Health Office)
- Proof of residency (for Bacolod residents)

### Process:

1. Visit **City Treasurer's Office** or market administration
2. Inquire about available stalls
3. Submit application and requirements
4. Pay stall rental deposit and advance
5. Sign lease agreement
6. Receive stall assignment
7. Set up business

### Rental Fees:

- **Daily rental:** ₱50-₱200 per day (depending on location and size)
- **Monthly rental:** ₱1,500-₱5,000 per month
- **Deposit:** 1-2 months advance rental

**Note:** Fees vary by market, stall size, and location within market.

---

## 3. Market Rules and Regulations

**Stall Holders Must:**

- Pay rental fees on time
- Maintain cleanliness of stall
- Follow market hours (usually 5 AM - 6 PM)
- Comply with health and sanitation standards
- Display business permit and sanitary permit
- Sell only permitted goods
- Follow fire safety regulations

**Prohibited:**

- Subletting stalls without permission
- Selling prohibited or illegal goods
- Blocking aisles or passageways
- Unsanitary practices
- Price manipulation or hoarding

---

## 4. LGU-Managed Cemeteries

**Public Cemeteries in Bacolod:**

- Contact **City government** for list of public cemeteries
- Some barangays have community cemeteries

**Services Available:**

- Burial plots (purchase or lease)
- Interment services
- Maintenance of cemetery grounds

---

## 5. How to Secure Cemetery Plot

### Requirements:

- Death certificate of deceased
- Valid IDs of family representative
- Proof of relationship to deceased
- Barangay clearance

### Process:

1. Visit **City government office** or cemetery administration
2. Inquire about available plots
3. Submit requirements
4. Pay plot fees
5. Receive plot assignment
6. Coordinate burial schedule

### Fees:

- Plot fees vary by cemetery and location
- Contact cemetery administration for current rates

## 7. Important Reminders

### For Market Stalls:

- **Secure permits first** - business and sanitary permits required
- **Pay on time** - late payments may result in stall forfeiture
- **Maintain cleanliness** - health inspections are regular
- **Follow market rules** - violations may result in penalties
- **Renew annually** - permits and rentals must be renewed

### For Cemetery Services:

- **Plan ahead** - secure plot before need arises
- **Keep documents** - save receipts and plot assignment papers
- **Maintain plot** - family responsible for plot maintenance
- **Follow cemetery rules** - respect cemetery regulations and hours
